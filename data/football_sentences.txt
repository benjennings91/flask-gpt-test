Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is the most popular sport in the world.
It is played by two teams of eleven players each on a rectangular field with a goal at each end.
The main objective is to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's net.
Players are not allowed to use their hands or arms, except for the goalkeeper within their penalty area.
A standard football match consists of two halves, each lasting 45 minutes, with a short halftime break.
Major international tournaments include the FIFA World Cup, UEFA European Championship, and Copa America.
Famous footballers like Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Pele have inspired millions around the globe.
The sport promotes teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness.
Football is played and loved by people of all ages in nearly every country.
Whether on a professional pitch or a street corner, the spirit of the game brings people together.
